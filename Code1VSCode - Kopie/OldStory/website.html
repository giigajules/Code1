Having escaped the Binary Dungeon, Carlos Weber's journey is far from over. As he steps out into the sunlight, he notices the air smells a little different, and the ground beneath his feet is softer.
An old man with fluffy hair and a red beard comes up to him, holding a set of playing cards.
"Do you want to play Durak?," he asks, eagerly awaiting Carlos' response.

"Let's play a round."

"No, please leave me alone."
